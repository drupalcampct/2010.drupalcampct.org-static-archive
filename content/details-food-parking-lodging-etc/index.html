The details - food, parking, lodging, etc.

Welcome to DrupalCamp-CT located at Luce Hall on the Yale University Campus. This page provides you with everything you need to know to get here, stay here and eat here. Breakfast and lunch will be provided giving you more time to meet fellow Drupalists and exchange ideas.


Food - We were able to work with some local restaurants to provide great food for Breakfast and Lunch. Bruegger's Bagels has generously provided coffee, tea etc. and of course, bagels for breakfast.


Abate's Pizza will be proving lunch. New Haven is known for it's pizza and Abate's is one of the BEST pizza restaurants in town.  


Getting to Luce Hall - Out of town guests can find Luce Hall using  this map and directions.


Parking - There are many options for parking throughout the campus in either Yale Parking Lots or on the street. lots located . Parking is free in Yale Lots on Saturdays; on-street parking is not so you will need quarters. 


There is a small Yale Lot on Trumbull Street and on-street parking and which will most likely fill up fast. Map and directions. There are also several larger Yale Lots a few blocks from Luce Hall on Whitney Ave. including the Peabody Museum, Kline Geology and 221 Whitney Ave - Map and directions 


Overnight Accomodations - For those of you from out of town who might want to stay overnight, this list of hotels should be helpful. http://www.yale.edu/visitor/lodging.html


Need more information? If you need additional information, please contact Nancy at nancy.flowers-mangs@yale.edu
