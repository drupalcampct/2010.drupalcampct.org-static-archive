Welcome to DrupalCampCT

DrupalCampCT is a full-day, no-cost event, structured around the open-source content management system, Drupal, which includes knowledgeable speakers, informative workshops, impromptu birds-of-a-feather sessions, and invaluable opportunities to network with other Drupalists in and around the Nutmeg State! We expect attendees to be a good mix of experienced Drupalists and newcomers to Drupal; developers, themers, content managers and project leaders; and consultants, corporate professionals, academics and passionate hobbists.  

The Details... parking, food, overnight accomodations

DrupalCamp-CT is located at Luce Hall on the Yale University Campus. Everything you need to know to get here, stay here and eat here can be found on the Details Page.  Breakfast and lunch will be provided giving you more time to meet fellow Drupalists and exchange ideas.

Getting to Luce Hall - Out of town guests can find Luce Hall using  this map and directions.

Parking - There are many options for parking throughout the campus in either Yale Parking Lots or on the street. lots located . Parking is free in Yale Lots on Saturdays; on-street parking is not so you will need quarters. 

There is a small Yale Lot on Trumbull Street and on-street parking and which will most likely fill up fast. Map and directions. There are also several larger Yale Lots a few blocks from Luce Hall on Whitney Ave. including the Peabody Museum, Kline Geology and 221 Whitney Ave - Map and directions
